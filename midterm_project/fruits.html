Food culture

Tropical fruits hold a significant position in various local cuisines, such as the Thai sticky rice with mango and the Brazilian fruit jelly. These have enriched the local dietary characteristics.

Tropical fruits are important economic crops in many tropical countries, such as bananas in the Philippines and durians in Thailand, which have driven the local economic growth.
